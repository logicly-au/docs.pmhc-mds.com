PMHC MDS Documentation
======================

The PMHC MDS is used by a range of users.

PHN and Service Providers Documentation
---------------------------------------

Documentation relevant to PMHC MDS users at Primary Health Networks and Service 
Providers is available in the :ref:`phn-po-documentation` section.

Third Party Developers Documentation
------------------------------------

Documentation relevant to developers of Client Information Management Systems (CIMS) who
are developing components to interact with the PMHC MDS, such as files to upload data
from their CIMS is available in the :ref:`third-party-developers` section.
